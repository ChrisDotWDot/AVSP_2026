AVSP 2026

The 16th International Conference on Auditory-Visual Speech Processing (AVSP2026) will be held in Sydney, Australia, October 2, 2026. AVSP is a satellite workshop of Interspeech 2026.

About AVSP

AVSP is a uniquely interdisciplinary conference, focusing on the effects of auditory and visual speech information on human perception, machine recognition, and human-machine interaction. AVSP conferences attract many researchers from various fields, such as psychology, computer engineering, neuroscience, linguistics, and robotic engineering.

Conference Venue

The conference will be held in the beautiful city of Sydney, Australia, known for its iconic landmarks, vibrant culture, and world-class research institutions. More details about the specific venue will be announced soon.

Program

The conference program will feature keynote presentations, oral and poster sessions, and opportunities for networking with researchers from around the world. Detailed program information will be available closer to the conference date.

Call for Papers

We invite submissions on all aspects of auditory-visual speech processing, including but not limited to:

Human Perception

Audiovisual speech perception, integration mechanisms, and multisensory processing

Machine Recognition

Audio-visual speech recognition, multimodal learning, and deep learning approaches

Neuroscience

Neural mechanisms, brain imaging studies, and cognitive processes

Applications

Human-machine interaction, assistive technologies, and real-world systems
